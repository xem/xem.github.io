Mini Unicode

December 2015 - October 2017

A collection of mini Unicode slideshows (from 54b to 347kb)

Golfed in 2015 and enhanced during #golfctober 2017.

I also golfed a 1kb version with a menu allowing to reach any block and submitted it to JS1K in 2016.

More info on Github

Scribblenauts golf-play

June 2018

I started playing the DS game Scribblenauts while trying to use the least number of objects and letters possible.

I first downloaded a list of all the words in the game, then beat every puzzle level of the worlds 1 to 9 with the fewest possible short words, sometimes in very long and hacky ways.

The description of each video contains the detailed stats for each level and many remarks on how the puzzles were solved.

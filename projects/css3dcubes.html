CSS3D cubes

October - December 2016

A hack/experiment allowing to display 25 CSS3D cubes despite Firefox's 100 CSS3D polygons limit.

It was finally used in a real advent calendar for my daily job.

(Firefox finally allows more than 100 3D elements since 2017)
